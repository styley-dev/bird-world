# Bird World - Explore the Skies

A fully explorable bird world game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.

## How to Play

1. Open in any modern web browser
2. Use **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** to control your bird
3. Hold **Space** for a speed boost
4. Explore different areas and collect seeds to increase your score!

## Features

- **Fully explorable world** (4000x4000 pixels)
- **6 unique areas** to discover:
  - Sky - Start your journey here
  - Forest - Dense trees and nature
  - Mountain - Snow-capped peaks
  - Ocean - Animated waves
  - Desert - Sandy dunes
  - Volcano - Erupting lava particles

- **Interactive elements**:
  - 100 collectible seeds scattered throughout the world
  - Animated bird with wing flapping
  - Smooth camera following system
  - Minimap showing your position and areas

- **Beautiful graphics**:
  - Animated clouds drifting through the sky
  - Ocean waves with smooth animation
  - Desert dunes with texture
  - Lava particles from the volcano
  - Smooth area transitions with gradient blending

## Controls

- **Arrow Keys / WASD**: Move in any direction
- **Space**: Speed boost
- The bird will automatically rotate to face the direction of movement

## Game Objectives

- Explore all 6 different areas
- Collect as many seeds as possible
- See how high you can get your score!

Enjoy exploring the Bird World!
